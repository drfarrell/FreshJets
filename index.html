FreshJets

A refresh of the private jet industry

Abstract

FreshJets is a startup based in Denver, CO that delivers private air transportation at first-class prices. This was an attempt to articulate a redesign concept now that the company has reached a strong userbase and is looking to raise funding soon for rapid growth.


...shifting the brand away from the stereotypical flat-look that startups tend to have.

Process


The only constraints for this redesign were to use the preexisting logo and create a strong ethos for attracting high-end customers, which I interpretted as shifting the brand away from the stereotypical flat-look that startups tend to have. In otherwords, to establish trust in the company and emphasize tasteful, forward-thinking design.

The logo has a serif monogram of "fj" and a sans serif title, so a font pairing that used both styles was ideal. Open Sans and PT Serif turned out to be a great pairing for the job. A light blue was the initial accent color, but I decided to use that specific hue for distinguishing a different division of the company (to be uploaded in a different project). I also found that blue is very common in airline branding, and considering that most photos of air travel will feature some portion of sky, flat gold would be a unique choice. I initially worried that the flat gold would be too close to getting "lost" on the white background, but it looks great on every shade of gray yet is still legible on a flat white.

This was mostly a visual redesign of their existing copy and photography for the explanation of their product.
